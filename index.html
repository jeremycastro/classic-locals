About Local Classics

Local Classics is a loyalty program designed for local businesses and the customers who love them. Our mission is to strengthen communities by making it easy and rewarding to shop local.

How It Works

1. Sign Up

Join Local Classics at any participating business or through our website.

2. Shop & Earn

Shop at your favorite local stores and earn points for every purchase.

3. Get Rewards

Redeem your points for discounts, exclusive products, and special offers.

For Businesses
